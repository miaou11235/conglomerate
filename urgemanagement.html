Urge Management

When you have the urge to engage in an unhealthy behavior, delay for a set period of time instead of immediately giving in to the urge. Follow these instructions:

  1. Rate the intensity of the urge from 1 to 10.
  2. Set a timer for 15 minutes. Wait until after the timer is up to gratify the urge.
  3. During the 15 minutes:
    1. Write out the pros and cons of following through with the behavior.
    2. Do an activity to distract yourself.
  4. After the 15 minutes are over, rate the intensity of the urge again.
    1. If the intensity is lower, you can return to your day.
    2. If the intensity is the same or higher, go back to step 2.
