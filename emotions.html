The lowdown on emotions

  • Emotions are neither good nor bad, right nor wrong. Feelings just ARE. They exist. It is not helpful to judge your emotions.

  • There is a difference between having an emotion and doing something or acting on the emotion.

  • Emotions don't last forever. No matter what you're feeling, eventually, it will lift and another emotion will take its place.

  • When a strong emotion comes, you do not have to act on your feeling. All you need to do is recognize the emotion and feel it.

  • Emotions are not facts. When emotions are very powerful they feel just like the truth.

  • You cannot get rid of emotions because they serve important survival functions.

Emotions help us, even if it doesn't seem like it

  • Emotions help us communicate. They show in our body language and in our facial expression, letting others know when something is wrong.

  • Emotions help us overcome obstacles. They give us motivation to change things, whether in our external situation or in our mind.

  • Emotions give us information about our situation, like alarms when we feel like something isn't right.

Myths about emotions

  • There is a right way to feel in every situation: there is no right or wrong way to feel.

  • Letting others know that you are feeling bad is a weakness: talking to others about how you feel is a good thing to do.

  • Uncomfortable feelings are bad and destructive: everyone has uncomfortable feelings sometimes, but these are neither good nor bad and they will not hurt you unless you act on them.

  • Being emotional means being out of control: feeling an emotion is not the same as acting on that emotion.

  • All painful emotions are a result of a bad attitude: no emotion is right or wrong and you can't control all of your emotions with your attitude.

  • If others don't approve of how you are feeling, you shouldn't feel the way that you do: other people can't change your emotions, and your emotions are neither good nor bad.

  • Other people are the best judge of how I am feeling: no one is feeling what you are feeling besides you.

  • Painful emotions are not really important and should be ignored: emotions can be painful, but they do not last forever. You can't get rid of painful emotions besides by waiting for them to pass.
