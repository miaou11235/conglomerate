FAQ
Are trans people real?
Yes. You can see them.

Are they just doing it for attention?
No. Usually, people don't want attention for attention's sake.

Are trans people just mentally ill?
No, but they have a higher rate of suicide than the general population because their lives are often miserable.

What about all the weird genders that they're making up?
Non-binary people are not breaking a law or forcing you to change your gender. It's kind of weird that you care what words they use to describe themselves.

What about all the confusing pronouns that I'm supposed to learn?
They're not very confusing at all if you're willing to learn them. Plus, the use of neopronouns is declining, and the great majority of non-binary people opt for "they." You're unlikely to need to learn.

But "they" is not a singular pronoun!
Yawn.

Aren't trans people just overblowing it when they say that they need protection and stuff?
No. Statistically, many, many people want to hurt them.

Why do they care about what bathroom they use?
Why do you care about what bathroom they use?

Glossary
Cisgender (cis): (adj) comfortable with the gender assigned at birth; not transgender
Transgender (trans): (adj) not comfortable with the gender assigned at birth; not cisgender
Non-binary: (adj) a subset of trans people whose genders lay outside the male/female "binary;" neither male or female
Neopronoun: (noun) a personal pronoun that has been coined in recent history
