What is this site?

Help for Various Mental Woes is designed to help you help yourself when you are feeling bad.

If you are experiencing the urge to do an addictive behavior, you could try urge management.

If you are experiencing painful emotions, you could read about emotions.

If you need something to do, you could look for a distraction.

What is coping?

Coping is learning to tolerate distress without becoming overwhelmed. Coping is important for everyone for managing emotions, dealing with unpleasant people, and living through urges to engage in unhealthy activities of all kinds.

Kinds of coping

  • Distraction: doing an activity
  • Self-soothing: using your senses to become calmer
  • Imagery: imagining that you are somewhere calm
  • Urge management: delaying when you want to engage in an unhealthy behavior
  • Worry list: writing a list of everything you're worried about.
  • Calming colors: looking at calming colors.
