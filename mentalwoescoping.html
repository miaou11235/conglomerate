What is coping?

Coping is learning to tolerate distress without becoming overwhelmed. Coping is important for everyone for managing emotions, dealing with unpleasant people, and living through urges to engage in unhealthy activities of all kinds.

Kinds of coping

  • Distraction: doing an activity
  • Observation: noticing your surroundings and your breath
  • Imagery: imagining that you are somewhere calm
  • Urge management: delaying when you want to engage in an unhealthy behavior
