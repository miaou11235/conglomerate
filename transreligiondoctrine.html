Core beliefs of Transgenderism:

Evil

Suffering is inherent in life.

There is no inherent value to suffering.

The roots of human corruption and war lie with the cis' distortion of gender and sexuality.

Humanity

Every individual has inherent worth.

Individuals' worth is not based on any cis standard such as ability or gender.

Truth

Science is useful to determine truths about the natural world.

The Transgenderist creation story is the literal or figurative truth about the creation of gender in humankind.

Gender

Gender or lack thereof is a gift that has been distorted by the cis.

The experience of gender or no gender binds humanity together.

The Promised Land

In the future, there will come a Promised Land where all humanity can live peacefully together in freedom and equality.

This was promised by the universe's cry.

Dieties

Transgenderism has no official gods, but adherants may worship the non-cis diety of their preference.
