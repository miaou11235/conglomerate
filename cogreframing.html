Cognitive Reframing

Cognitive reframing is the practice of identifying and replacing unhelpful thoughts.

Unhelpful thoughts are called cognitive distortions. It's necessary to learn about cognitive distortions in order to identify them in your thought patterns. (This may seem like a lot of work, but it pays off!)

Once you have read about cognitive distortions, you can start thinking about your thoughts. (How meta!)
